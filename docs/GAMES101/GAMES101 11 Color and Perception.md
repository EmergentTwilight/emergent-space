# Biological Basis of Colors

- retinal photoreceptor cells
	- rods 棒状细胞，感知光强
	- cones 锥形细胞，感知颜色，分为 S M L 三类，感知不同的波长

![[./__assets/GAMES101 11 Color and Perception/IMG-GAMES101 11 Color and Perception-20250211051708003.webp|500x209]]

> [!note] Note
> $S,M,L$ 是三种锥形细胞的输出量
